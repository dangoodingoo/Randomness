Welcome to TimeShift

A passion project that lets you explore random snapshots of the early web (1996-2008).

Each jump queries the Internet Archive's database in real-time, searching through millions of archived pages to find you a random snapshot. Their API takes 3-10 seconds to respond. If the first search comes up empty, it tries different domains, which adds more wait time. Unlike sites like wiby.org that pre-cache their pages, this pulls fresh results every time you jump.

Every forgotten site is a whisper from another time.

Press SPACE or click the button to begin.
